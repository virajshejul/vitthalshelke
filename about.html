ABOUT ME

ALEXANDER NOIR NEW YORK, 2024

I am a visual storyteller driven by the pursuit of authenticity, capturing the essence of human emotion and the beauty of our world through a minimalist lens.

Based in New York, my journey into photography began over a decade ago, fueled by a fascination with light, shadow, and the spaces in between. My work explores the intersection of documentary and fine art, creating images that are both truthful and timeless.

With a background in visual arts and years of experience working with international brands and editorial publications, I've developed a distinctive aesthetic characterized by clean compositions, dramatic contrasts, and an unwavering attention to detail.

My approach is rooted in patience and observation. I believe that the most powerful photographs emerge when we allow moments to unfold naturally, when we become invisible observers of life's quiet poetry.
